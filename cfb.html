Healthy Chicken Fajita Bowls

This chicken fajita recipe is easy to put together and tastes great. It's also healthy to boot!

Recipe Source

Ingredients

For chicken and peppers

  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2-1 teaspoon ground chili (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro or parsley
  • 4 large chicken thighs
  • 2 large bell peppers

For the rice

  • 1/2 cup long grain rice (or Jasmine)
  • 1 cup chicken stock (or broth)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1-2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2-3 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro or parsley

For burrito bowl

  • 5 cups Romaine lettuce
  • 7 ounce (200 g) can black beans, washed and drained
  • 7 ounce (200 g) corn
  • 1/2 a red onion, chopped
  • 1 avacado peeled, seeded, and sliced

Dressing

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped cilantro or parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2-1 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine lime juice, oil, garlic, cumin, salt, chili, and cilantro/parsley. Mix together. Add the chicken thighs and let marinate for 30 minutes.

  2. While chicken is marinating, begin cooking rice. Combine together rice, chicken stock, and garlic in a pot on high heat. Mix well and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and cover with a lid until rice is cooked through.

  3. Heat olive oil in large skillet on medium-high heat. Sear chicken on both sides until golden. Flip them while cooking to get a good char. Tranfer chicken to a warm plate and cover with foil.

  4. While chicken and rice are cooking, whisk together ingredients for dressing.

  5. Add peppers to skillet. Drizzle with extra olive oil if needed. Cook until soft and slightly charred. Season with salt and pepper.

  6. When rice is done, fluff it work fork and mix with lime juice and cilantro/parsley. Season with salt and pepper.

  7. Combine lettuce, beans, corn, onion, and peppers into 4 bowls. Slice chicken into strips and arrange into bowls. Top each bowl with avacado slices and drizzle with dressing.

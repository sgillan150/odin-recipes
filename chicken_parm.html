Delicious chicken parmesean

This recipe will show you how to make fantastic chicken parmesean right at home!

One of the things that I really like about this recipe is that it you don't drown the chicken in sauce like you sometimes see at restaurants. You can also use whatever cheese you happen to ahve sitting at home. I often end up using cheddar instead of mozzerala if that's what I have on hand. Either way it turns out great!

The quantities listed are from the recipe source. Many of the ingredients do not need to be measured out in the exact quantity listed. In these cases I have added a note saying to use as needed.

Recipe source: allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breats
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • Seasoned panko bread crumbs - ~1 cup. Pour as needed to cover chicken
  • Parmesan cheese - ~3/4 cup. Grated as required to cover chicken
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (optional)
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Tomato sauce - ~1/2 cup. Use as needed to cover the chicken
  • Mozzeralla - ~1/4 cup freshly grated. Use as needed to cover chicken
  • Basil - ~1/4 cup. Use as needed to cover the chicken
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese (Optional)

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 450 F (230 C)
  2. Place chicken between sheets of plastic. Pound chicken to a thickness of ~1/2 inch with smooth side of a meet mallet (or frying pan)
  3. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  4. Beat eggs in bowl
  5. Mix bread crumbs and 1/2 cup parmesean cheese in a separate bowl
  6. Sprinkle flour on chicken breasts (optional)
  7. Dip chicken in eggs and thoroughly coat both sides. Transfer chicken to bread crumbs. Press bread crumbs to both sides. Repeat for each breast. Let chicken rest 10 - 15 minutes
  8. Heat olive oil in large skillet on medium-high heat. Cook chicken in oil until golden, about 2 minutes per side. The chicken will finish cooking in the oven. Work in batches if needed.
  9. Transfer chicken to baking dish. Top each breast with tomato sauce, mozzerala, basil, provolone (optional), and parmesean. Drizzle 1/2 teaspoon olive oil on each
  10. Bake until chicken is fully cooked (165 F or 74 C), about 15 - 20 minutes.

Tasty Honey Garlic Pork Chops

The sauce for these pork chops tastes just like honey garlic wing sauce. It's a wonderful combination and very easy to put together!

Recipe Source

Ingredients
  • 4 Pork chops
  • Salt and pepper to season
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup water (or chicken broth)
  • 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar (or any white vinegar)

Instructions
  1. Heat oil in pan over medium high heat
  2. Sear chops on both sides until golden and cooked through (about 4 - 5 minutes each side). Transfer to a plate and set aside
  3. Reduce heat to medium. Heat butter in same pan, scraping up brown bits. Saute garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add honey, water, and vinegar. Increase heat to medium-high and continue to cook until the sauce reduces down and thickens slightly (about 3 - 4 minutes) while stirring occaisionally.
  5. Add pork back into pan. Base with sauce and grill for 1 - 2 minutes until edges are slightly charred.
  6. Garnish with parsely.
